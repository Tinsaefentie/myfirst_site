Starting My Path in Frontend Development

Tech wasn't my first love. I entered this field amidst societal pressures, but I've since found its creative potential. My goal is to build interfaces that are simple, intuitive, and make technology feel invisible.

Currently, I'm diving into the world of HTML and CSS. I'm not an expert yet, but every line of code I write is a step forward. My focus right now is on learning the fundamentals, experimenting, and figuring out how to craft experiences that reduce friction and empower users.

Although I'm just beginning, I hope to use this platform to document my learning and connect with others on the same journey. I want to be a part of a community where we help each other grow, even if we're just starting out.
